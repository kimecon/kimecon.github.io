Welcome!

I am an Associate Professor in the Management Department at the University of Memphis. As a corporate governance scholar, I examine how psychological, sociopolitical, and institutional dynamics shape organizational decision-making and firm outcomes. My research focuses on the intersection of board diversity, CEO succession, and shareholder activism.

My work has been published in Strategic Management Journal, Journal of Management, Journal of Management Studies, Strategic Organization, and Corporate Governance: An International Review, among others. I also serve on the editorial review boards of Journal of Management and Journal of Management Studies.
